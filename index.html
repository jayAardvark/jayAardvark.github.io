About Me

I am a recent graduate of UCSD (2018) where I enjoyed—battled!—my way through what I like to think of as problem-solving training. Countless hours were spent staring at pages uncomprehendingly, non-working brain flickering like a lightbulb about to go out, until at last I found elusive solutions! This “problem-solving training” was presented to me in many different forms—from calculus to grammatically parsing ancient Latin texts to be translated into English to puzzle-like programming challenges in MatLab. And so, perhaps, one way that I can categorize myself is as a (Fairly) Diverse Problem-Solver.

Another significant category that I think I fall under is as a Music Lover. Not only do I enjoy writing and recording music as a hobby, but music has also led me into an electronics hobby—I enjoy, for example, modifying the electronics in my guitars and building effects pedals and working on vintage vacuum tube amplifiers.

All of the about-me stuff above has led to the creation of this work-in-progress page along with the apps that I’ve built that are presented here. I have studied HTML, CSS, and JavaScript as a sort of curiosity for a while now but up until recently I haven’t touched any frameworks such as React. The apps that I’ve built and present here showcase my growing relationship with React as I learn by doing in React.

Contact me if you could use a "(Fairly) Diverse Problem-Solver" to help you achieve your goals!
